## Project Overview

**Olist E-commerce Analysis and Report** is a comprehensive data analysis project focused on Brazil's Olist e-commerce platform. The project examines sales data, customer behavior, logistics, and market trends to provide actionable business insights.

### Objectives
- Analyze Olist's sales performance and revenue drivers
- Identify customer demographics and purchasing patterns
- Optimize delivery logistics and costs
- Improve customer satisfaction through review analysis
- Generate data-driven recommendations for business growth

---

## Methodology

The analysis follows a structured, multi-step approach:

### 1. Data Modeling
- Import raw data into Excel and Power BI
- Clean and transform data into a coherent structure
- Create relationships between datasets for comprehensive analysis

### 2. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Identify trends and patterns in sales data
- Discover outliers and anomalies
- Use pivot tables and visualization techniques

### 3. Statistical Analysis
- Apply regression analysis
- Calculate trend metrics
- Generate quantitative insights

### 4. Insights Generation
- Synthesize findings from EDA and statistical analysis
- Extract actionable business intelligence

### 5. Recommendations Development
- Translate insights into strategic recommendations
- Focus on operational optimization and profitability

## Recommendations

### 1. Payment Strategy Optimization
- Enhance credit card payment experience
- Analyze Boleto performance drivers
- Maintain and grow both payment methods

### 2. Logistics & Delivery
- Investigate high-cost product categories
- Optimize delivery route planning
- Negotiate better carrier rates
- Adjust pricing to account for delivery expenses

### 3. Pricing & Revenue Management
- Implement dynamic pricing strategies
- Launch targeted promotions during peak seasons
- Optimize product mix by season
- Monitor monthly pricing trends

### 4. Geographic Expansion
- Focus investments in high-potential regions like S√£o Paulo
- Develop targeted strategies for underperforming regions (e.g., Esp√≠rito Santo)
- Consider region-specific marketing and product selection

### 5. Customer Experience
- Maintain consistent, high-quality customer feedback
- Address factors affecting monthly review score fluctuations
- Enhance overall customer experience
- Streamline the review process

### 6. Order Optimization
- Analyze pricing for "Unavailable" and "Canceled" orders
- Investigate delivery fee variations by order status
- Reduce unproductive order types
- Maintain profitability for approved orders
